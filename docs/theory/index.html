Music theory in BQN

  • Scales are the groups of notes used as "context" in most music.
  • Tertian scales include the standard major and minor, many useful variations, and some oddities like the whole-tone scale.
  • Tertian scale modulation describes the structure of 7-note tertian scales in terms of which can modulate to others by changing a single note.
  • Symmetric scales, a note about scales that repeat periodically.
